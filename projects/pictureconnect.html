Picture Connect

The purpose of Picture Connect is to give individuals an outlet to share their personal or creative photos. The main functionality is the powerful photo sorting algorithm with the assistance of user inputted tags to clearly define and allocate other photos that the user may find similar or interesting.

Helping users find amazing photos with similar tastes helps to broaden the horizon for anyone to have a creative input on the topic. Whether it be sightseeing, home decorations or cars, anyone can find a niche hobby or interest with a plethora of popular and unseen photos.
